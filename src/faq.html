FAQs

General Questions

  1. What is Clumbee? Clumbee is a nightlife companion app that helps you discover nightclubs and events while offering exclusive features like group interactions, chat functionalities, and exclusive offers. It’s designed to enhance and simplify your nightlife experience.
  2. Who can use Clumbee? Clumbee is designed for users who are at least 18 years old. By accessing the app, you confirm that you meet this age requirement and agree to comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  3. How can I register on Clumbee? You can create an account on Clumbee using your phone number with OTP verification.

Account and Security

  1. How do I reset my password? Clumbee uses OTP-based login, so you don’t need to remember a password. Simply request a new OTP if you need to log in again.
  2. What should I do if I suspect unauthorized activity on my account? If you suspect any unauthorized activity, immediately log out from all devices and contact us at support@clumbee.com for assistance.
  3. How can I delete my Clumbee account? You can delete your account directly within the Clumbee app. Navigate to the account settings, press the "Delete Account" button, and confirm your action. Your account will be permanently deleted.

Features and Functionality

  1. What services does Clumbee provide?
    • Nightclub and event information.
    • Exclusive offers, discounts, and club advertisements.
    • Search and filter options by location, ratings, and more.
    • Group creation (public, private, gender-specific, or mixed).
    • Chat functionalities within groups.
  2. Can I create my own groups on Clumbee? Yes, Clumbee allows you to create public or private groups, including specialized types like male-only, female-only, couples, or mixed groups.
  3. Are there guidelines for group interactions? Yes, group admins are responsible for moderating content and members in their groups. Users must comply with community guidelines and refrain from posting inappropriate or harmful content.
  4. How do the exclusive offers work? Exclusive offers and discounts are provided by third-party clubs. While Clumbee facilitates these offers, we are not responsible for their validity or fulfillment. Please confirm details directly with the clubs.

Privacy and Data Security

  1. What personal information does Clumbee collect? Clumbee collects:
    • Phone number for account registration.
    • Profile and gallery images (if uploaded).
    • Location data (if enabled) for proximity-based recommendations.
    • Interactions with clubs, events, and groups.
  2. Is my data safe with Clumbee? Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information. However, no transmission method is completely secure, so we encourage users to remain cautious.
  3. Does Clumbee share my data with third parties? Clumbee does not sell personal data. We may share anonymized data with clubs and use service providers to help operate the platform.

Technical Support

  1. I’m facing issues with the app. How do I get help? For technical support, contact us at support@clumbee.com with details of the issue you’re experiencing.
  2. Why can’t I see club offers or events in my area? Club offers and events are dependent on participating clubs. If you’re not seeing offers, it may be due to limited participation in your region.
  3. How do I manage app notifications? You can manage notifications through the app settings or your device’s notification preferences.

Legal and Terms

  1. What happens if I violate the Terms and Conditions? Clumbee reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate its Terms and Conditions.
  2. Can the Terms and Conditions change? Yes, Clumbee’s Terms and Conditions may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted, and continued use of the app implies acceptance of the updated terms.
  3. How is my location data used? Location data is used to provide proximity-based club recommendations and enhance your overall experience. You can disable location services at any time through your device settings.
  4. Who should I contact for further questions or concerns? For any queries, please reach out to us at support@clumbee.com.
